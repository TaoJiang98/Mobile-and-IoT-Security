Instructions for receiving payment

 

GENERAL PAYMENT INFORMTION:

·         Do not include any personally identifiable information in your emails. Note that even if you do accidentally send identifying information, we will not store it or use it in any way.

·         Forwarded calls must be completed by 07 December to count towards a potential bonus payout.

o   If you send an email to us after 15 December, you may not receive your bonus payout. Please email us before then.

o   We will, however, honor the initial $4 payment even if you email us after 15 December.

·         See below for further instructions on what to include in your emails.

 

OFFLINE PARTICIPANTS (i.e. Non-prolific participants):

To receive compensation:

·         Email mrcrobocalltranscripts@gmail.com

o   Set the subject header to “Compensation.”

o   In the body of the email, include:

§  Participant ID that you entered into the survey’s google form

§  Number of additional calls you forwarded (for bonus payments)

·         Remember not to include the first call you forwarded, as that is required for the initial $4 payout.

§  Preferred payment method. We offer the following choices:

·         PayPal

·         Venmo

·         Gift Card to restaurant/respected place of business of your choice

§  If you prefer either of the first two options, please provide us with your account information so we may wire the money to you.

·         We will not use this information for anything beyond this purpose.

§  If you prefer the gift card option, please tell us from where to get it (chain businesses like Subway or Taco Bell are preferred)

·         We will reply to you with a digital gift card to that place for the amount you are owed

o   Once your survey and forwarded calls are verified, we will send you your payment through the method of your choice

 

PROLIFIC PARTICIPANTS:

To receive compensation:

·         Send us a Prolific message

o   Set the subject header or first line of the body to “Compensation.”

o   In the body of the message, include:

§  Your Prolific ID (if it is not already included elsewhere)

§  Number of additional calls you forwarded (for bonus payments).

·         Remember not to include the first call you forwarded, as that is required for the initial $4 payout.

o   Once your survey and forwarded calls are verified, we will respond to you with a completion code that you may enter manually in the Prolific app to claim the $4.

o   We will also award you with your bonus payout, if applicable, through the Prolific platform. We will do this manually on or around 15 December.
